Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exciting action-packed survival game where you must defeat waves of monsters while upgrading your character's abilities and weapons. Test your skills and see how long you can survive!

The game features dynamic combat mechanics, multiple character classes, and a wide variety of monsters to battle. Each playthrough offers a unique experience with procedurally generated enemy waves and power-ups.

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless battle for survival. How long can you last?

Character Progression

Collect experience and upgrade your character with powerful abilities and weapons as you play.

Dynamic Combat

Engage in fast-paced combat with responsive controls and strategic gameplay elements.

Multiple Challenges

Encounter various monster types, each with unique abilities and attack patterns to overcome.

How to Play

  • Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Attacks: Your character attacks automatically at nearby enemies
  • Upgrades: Collect experience orbs from defeated monsters to level up
  • Power-ups: Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when leveling up
  • Survival: Avoid getting overwhelmed and survive as long as possible!
